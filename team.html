MEET OUR TEAM

MOTSWAI MOTSWAI
Mr. Motswai Motswai
Founder & Director – Tataaza Okavango Adventures

Mr. Motswai Motswai is an experienced tourism professional and entrepreneur born in Gonotshoga and raised in Maun, Botswana's tourism hub. His career began at Desert & Delta Safaris (1982–1984) as a waiter, where he developed a strong foundation in customer service.

He later worked in sales at BIG (1985–1990), gaining valuable business and client relations experience. From 1990 to 2006, he served as an Operations Manager at Engine, where he built extensive expertise in operations, leadership, and service delivery.

Since 2006, Mr. Motswai has been an entrepreneur, culminating in the establishment of Tataaza Okavango Adventures. With decades of experience, he is committed to delivering authentic, high-quality travel experiences while promoting Botswana as a premier safari destination.

NAMETSO JAEL NKETSO
Nametso Jael Nketso
Co-Founder & Partner – Tataaza Okavango Adventures

Nametso Jael Nketso is a dynamic professional with a strong background in customer service, administration, and tourism management. She began her career at Absa (2007–2020), where she built extensive experience in customer relations, service excellence, and client engagement.

In 2022, she served as a Legal Secretary at Dingake Law Partners, gaining valuable administrative and organizational expertise. She later transitioned into the tourism sector as General Manager at Honey Guide Adventures Trust (2022–2023), where she played a key role in operations management and delivering exceptional travel experiences.

Since 2023, Nametso has been an entrepreneur and co-founder of Tataaza Okavango Adventures, where she brings her diverse experience, leadership, and passion for service to help drive the company's growth and commitment to excellence.
